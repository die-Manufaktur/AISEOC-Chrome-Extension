About Ranked Choice Voting Maryland

We're a grassroots organization dedicated to bringing fairer, more representative elections to Maryland through ranked choice voting.

Our Mission

Ranked Choice Voting Maryland (RCVMD) is committed to advancing electoral reform that ensures every voice is heard and every vote counts. We believe that ranked choice voting creates a more inclusive democracy where candidates must appeal to a broader range of voters, reducing negative campaigning and promoting civil discourse.

Our team works tirelessly to educate voters, engage with legislators, and build a coalition of supporters who believe in fairer elections for all Marylanders.

Our Core Values

🗳️

Democracy

We believe in strengthening democratic participation and ensuring every voter has a meaningful say in who represents them.

🤝

Inclusion

RCV ensures all voices are heard, reducing barriers and promoting diverse representation in government.

✨

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and a commitment to evidence-based advocacy for electoral reform.
