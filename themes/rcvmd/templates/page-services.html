How We Serve Maryland

From education to advocacy, we provide multiple pathways for Marylanders to engage with ranked choice voting and electoral reform.

Voter Education

We provide comprehensive educational resources and workshops to help Maryland voters understand how ranked choice voting works and why it matters.

  • Interactive workshops and presentations
  • Educational materials and guides
  • Community town halls
  • Online resources and videos
Learn More

Legislative Advocacy

We work directly with Maryland lawmakers to advance ranked choice voting legislation at the local and state levels.

  • Bill drafting and policy research
  • Legislative testimony
  • Coalition building with elected officials
  • Tracking and analyzing legislation
View Legislation

Community Organizing

Building grassroots support across Maryland through local chapters, events, and volunteer opportunities.

  • Local chapter support and coordination
  • Volunteer training programs
  • Community events and rallies
  • Petition drives and signature collection
